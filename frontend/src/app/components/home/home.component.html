Features

Here are some of the features that are offered by PrIde.

Identity Verification

Credentials can be issued and validated on the blockchain.

Data Integrity

Integrity and Authenticity of credentials is maintained by the blockchain.

Self Sovereign Identity

User has complete control over the data to be shared with third parties.

Zero-knowledge Proof

Prove required identity information without revealing any other information.
